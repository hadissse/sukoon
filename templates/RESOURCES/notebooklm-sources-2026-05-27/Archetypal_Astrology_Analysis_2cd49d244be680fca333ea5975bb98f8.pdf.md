# Introduction to Archetypal Astrology 

### This is an archetypal astrological analysis of a natal chart and transits for many important events in a life. The analysis explores the planetary archetypes, their relationships, and how they manifest in work and spiritual development. 

## Understanding the Archetypal Perspective 

### The archetypal astrological perspective operates primarily as a spiritual discipline. This can be a disciplined practice engaging both our percepts and understanding of archetypal experiences. 

### Archetypes can be understood in multiple ways: 

### Homeric gods and goddesses (original Western vision) 

### Platonic forms or ideas 

### Aristotelian universals (formal, efficient, material, and final causes) 

### Jungian psychological complexes or Hillman's "gods of the psyche" 

### Freudian or Darwinian instincts 

### Whitehead's Eternal objects or Rupert Sheldrake's morphic fields 

### Quantum fields 

### Spiritual Beings 

### What's reflected in different thinkers' perceptions of archetypes is the evolution of consciousness itself.
