Biographical information

Melanie Reinhart is best known for her book 'Chiron and the Healing Journey', and her work on Chiron and the Centaurs Chariklo, Nessus and Pholus. She is a prize-winning diploma holder of the Faculty of Astrological Studies in the UK (Margaret Hone Award 1979), of whom she is also a patron. She was awarded the prestigious Charles Harvey Award in 2004, given by the Astrological Association of Great Britain for 'exceptional service to astrology'. Her work is an unusual combination of intuition and meticulous research.

Melanie was born in Zimbabwe, whose night sky inspired her vocation of astrology at an early age; she began studying the ancient Wisdom Traditions at the age of ten. She holds a B.A. Degree (University of Cape Town) in English, Music and Drama, and has been a professional astrologer since 1975, this being a natural extension of her interest in soul, consciousness and the spiritual life. She has wide experience of many psychotherapeutic approaches and spiritual traditions, and has studied with a number of fine teachers. 

 Melanie was initially self-taught in astrology, immersing herself for many years in the work of Dane Rudhyar. Later she had the privilege of learning from the many gifted astrologers in the rich milieu of London, and that journey continues. Melanie studied Horary astrology with Geoffrey Cornelius (1989-1990), and Deborah Houlding (Practitioner's Certificate 2012).

Melanie has taught for many highly esteemed astrology schools, in the UK and abroad, including the [Centre for Psychological Astrology](http://www.cpalondon.com/)[,](http://www.cpalondon.com/) the [London School of Astrology,](http://www.londonschoolofastrology.co.uk/) the [Faculty of Astrological Studies,](http://www.astrology.org.uk/)[Aula Astrològica de Catalunya](http://www.astrology.org.uk/), [Heaven and Earth Workshops](https://www.heavenandearthworkshops.com/) and [Astro*Synthesis.](http://www.astrosynthesis.com.au/) She has presented astrological seminars, lectures and experiential programs for local astrology groups in the UK, eight European countries, South Africa, USA, Mexico, Australia, New Zealand, Hong Kong and Taiwan.  Currently living in rural Bedfordshire, Melanie maintains a busy consulting practice with an international  clientele.  This intimate use of astrology as a vehicle for personal guidance is the heart and soul of her astrological work. 

She ran her own workshop programme, based mainly in London, from 1991-2015, and has recently begun offering online webinars hosted by various astrological organisations including the [Faculty of Astrological Studies](https://www.astrology.org.uk/online-seminars/) and the [Astrology University](https://www.astrologyuniversity.com/category/search-by-astrologer/melanie-reinhart/). You will also find a number of interviews with her on YouTube. In addition, she has written a number of highly acclaimed books which have been translated into several languages; her own publishing imprint is ['Starwalker Press'](https://melaniereinhart.com/starwalkerpress). Many articles archived on this site were originally (or subsequently) published in printed magazines such as '[Link Up](http://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/obituaries/9363131/Shauna-Crockett-Burrows.html)' (no longer in print), ['Caduceus'](http://www.caduceus.info), [The Mountain Astrologer](http://mountainastrologer.com/tma/), and the Journal of the [Astrological Association of Great Britain.](http://www.astrologicalassociation.co.uk/) Online you will also find her work on [astro.com ](http://www.astro.com)and [I.A.M. magazine.](https://infinityastrologicalmagazine.com/melanie-reinhart-london-united-kingdom/)

Melanie's personal and professional work is under the supervision of a senior teacher in the [Ridhwan School, ](https://www.ridhwan.org/)and she
